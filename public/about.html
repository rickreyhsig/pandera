Our History

Tenleytown Lawn & Landscape has been serving clients in landscape design and lawn care for over 15 years. A native Washingtonian and the founder of Tenleytown, Peter Galvin grew up in the area and operates locally, serving DC, Northern Virginia and parts of Montgomery County such as Bethesda, Chevy Chase, and Potomac.


Why Choose Us

We service a wide range of customers from residential homes to embassies, gasoline stations, apartment buildings and other commercial properties. We're proud to note that all of our landscape technicians have been with Tenleytown for years, so they are experienced, committed and courteous. Our employees perform their work with efficiency and care.
